# Product Overview

Taxja is an automated tax management platform for Austrian taxpayers (employees, landlords, self-employed, small business owners).

## Core Features

- Transaction management with AI-powered classification
- Austrian tax calculations (2026 USP rates)
- OCR document recognition for receipts/invoices
- VAT and social insurance (SVS) calculations
- Multi-language support (German, English, Chinese)
- PWA mobile-first design

## Key Principles

- GDPR compliant with AES-256 encryption
- Reference system only - not official tax advice
- Final filing through FinanzOnline
- Complex cases require Steuerberater consultation

## Target Users

Austrian taxpayers needing automated tax tracking and calculation assistance.
